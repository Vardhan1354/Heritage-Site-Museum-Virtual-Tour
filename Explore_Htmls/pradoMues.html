Explore Around:Prado Museum (Museo Nacional del Prado)

Nearby Attractions

  • Reina Sofía Museum: Approximately a 10-minute walk away, this museum features modern and contemporary art, including Picasso's renowned "Guernica."
  • El Retiro Park: Adjacent to the museum, this expansive park offers beautiful gardens, sculptures, and a peaceful lake—perfect for a leisurely stroll after your museum visit.

Hotels Nearby

  • Radisson Blu Hotel, Madrid Prado: Situated just 0.13 miles from the museum, this hotel provides modern amenities and is within walking distance to major attractions.
  • Mercure Madrid Centro: Also 0.13 miles away, this hotel offers comfortable rooms and is praised for its convenient location near the Prado Museum.
