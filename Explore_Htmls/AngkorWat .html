Explore Around: Angkor Wat

Nearby Attractions

  • Bayon Temple: Known for its enigmatic smiling stone faces, located in the Angkor Thom complex.
  • Ta Prohm: Famous for the massive trees growing out of its ruins, offering a unique blend of nature and architecture.

Hotels Nearby

  • Sabara Angkor Resort & Spa: Offers spacious rooms with private plunge pools, located about a 10-minute drive from Angkor Wat.
  • Central Corner d'Angkor: A boutique hotel providing comfortable accommodations with easy access to local attractions.

Medical Services

  • Royal Angkor International Hospital

Local Tips

Dress Code: Wear modest clothing covering shoulders and knees when visiting temple

Sunrise Visits: Arrive early to witness the breathtaking sunrise over Angkor Wat.
