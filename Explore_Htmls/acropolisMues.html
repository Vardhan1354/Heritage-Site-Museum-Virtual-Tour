Explore Around: Acropolis Museum

Nearby Attractions

  • Parthenon: Just a 5-minute walk from the museum, this iconic temple atop the Acropolis hill is a must-see.
  • Plaka District: A charming neighborhood with cobblestone streets, shops, and eateries, situated near the museum.

Hotels Nearby

  • Acropolis View Hotel: Situated in the historical center, this hotel provides spectacular views of the Parthenon and is only 3 minutes from the museum.
  • AthensWas Design Hotel: Located just a minute's walk from the museum, this boutique hotel offers modern amenities and easy access to major sites.

Local Tips

Wear Comfortable Shoes: The area around the museum and Acropolis involves a fair amount of walking on uneven surfaces; comfortable footwear is advisable.
