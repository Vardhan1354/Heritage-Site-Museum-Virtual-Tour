  • The Brickyard Retreat at Mutianyu:A boutique hotel offering stunning views of the Great Wall, blending modern comfort with natural beauty.

Local Tips

Section Selection: Mutianyu is less crowded and offers a more authentic experience compared to Badaling.

Best Time to Visit: Spring (April-May) and autumn (September-October) provide pleasant weather and clear views.
