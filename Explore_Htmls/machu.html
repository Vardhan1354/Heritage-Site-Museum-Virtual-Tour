Explore Around:Machu Picchu

Nearby Attractions

  • Sacred Valley: A region encompassing picturesque villages, Incan ruins, and agricultural terraces.
  • Huayna Picchu: A mountain offering a challenging hike with panoramic views of Machu Picchu.

Hotels Nearby

  • Inkaterra Machu Picchu Pueblo Hotel: A luxury eco-resort nestled in the cloud forest, providing a serene retreat with nature trails.
  • Sumaq Machu Picchu Hotel: An upscale hotel offering Peruvian culinary experiences and cultural activities.

Medical Services

  • Clinica Machu Picchu

Local Tips

Altitude Acclimatization: Spend a few days in Cusco or the Sacred Valley to adjust to the altitude before visiting Machu Picchu.

Ticketing: Purchase entrance tickets and train tickets well in advance, as daily visitor numbers are limited.
